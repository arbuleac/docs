# Introduction

**HeyShield Faithguard** provides secure and manageable protection for your home or office network.\
This section introduces the HeyShield platform and how to get started.

## Key Features

- Centralized dashboard for managing devices and routers.
- Seamless integration across desktop and mobile platforms.
- Real-time monitoring and protection statistics.

## Prerequisites

Before starting:

- Ensure you have a registered HeyShield account.
- Have administrative access to your network devices.
