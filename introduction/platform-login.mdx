To \*\*login \*\*to HeyShield platform open your preferred browser and access the following URL:

[HeyShield platform](https://admin.heyvalue.com/)

Fill in your credentials (email and password) and click "Continue"

![Introduction Login 1 Pn](/images/introduction_login_1.png)

If credentials are valid you'll be redirected to HeyShield dashboard, composed of 2 sections:

a) "Protect" - current status of service and management of service and devices.

![Introduction Login 2 Pn](/images/introduction_login_2.png)

b) "Statistics" - updated statistics of service for last 30 days.

![Statistics Overview Pn](/images/statistics_overview.png)

To **logout** from HeyShield platform, click on "My account" link on upper right and select "Sign Out"

![Introduction Login 3 Pn](/images/introduction_login_3.png)

To **reset password**, from login page click on "Forgot password" and fill in your email address and click "Continue"

![Introduction Reset Password 1 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_1.png)

If email is valid and there is an HeyShield account associated to that email, you'll be notified that a message was sent to indicated email address

![Introduction Reset Password 2 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_2.png)

Message contains an URL with a temporary security token that allow you to enter a new password

![Introduction Reset Password 3 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_3.png)

In the reset password page, fill in the new password, confirm it and click "Reset password"

![Introduction Reset Password 4 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_4.png)

Password has some requirements to ensure that your account is not expossed to security risks.

![Introduction Reset Password 5 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_5.png)

A notification message will confirm that the password reset was successfull.

![Introduction Reset Password 6 Pn](/images/introduction_reset_password_6.png)
