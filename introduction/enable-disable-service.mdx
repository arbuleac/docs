---
title: "Enable/Disable service"
description: "Instructions to enable or disable service"
---

Go to HeyShield platform and select "Protect" section on left menu. On the section "Filtering ..." if swipe button on the right side has backgroun green, service is active. This is the default state of the service.

To disable the service swipe button to left.

![Introduction Enable Service 1 Pn](/images/introduction_enable_service_1.png)

A new pop-up windows will appear

![Introduction Enable Service 2 Pn](/images/introduction_enable_service_2.png)

Stopping content filtering has the following options:

- 30 minutes - content filtering will be automatically enabled after 30 minutes
- 1 hour - content filtering will be automatically enabled after 1 hour
- 2 hours - content filtering will be automatically enabled after 2 hours
- Until tomorrow - - content filtering will be automatically enabled after 24 hours
- Indefinetly - until content filtering is manually enabled again

![Introduction Enable Service 3 Pn](/images/introduction_enable_service_3.png)

Select desired option and then click "Stop". Swipe button has now red backgroud, meaning that the service is disabled, also text section is changed.

![Introduction Enable Service 4 Pn](/images/introduction_enable_service_4.png)

You can manually enable content filtering service at any moment.
